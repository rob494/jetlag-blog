I force myself to drink the entire 32oz bottle during every flight, refilling it twice on long-hauls. The difference in how I feel upon landing is dramatic.

**Pro tip:** Fill it with electrolyte mix (I use LMNT) for better absorption.

**Best for:** Everyone. This is foundational.
